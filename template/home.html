XamTrack-- Tracking Exams Easily


We here at XamTrack, have created a platform where tests can be conducted with multitier invigilation system.

Multitier Invigilation involves our smart trained AI which informs the exam-invigilator(human) about the suspected student. That makes the job easy for conduction of online examination.

How it works

Create an account

Teacher and students both have to create a account and after teacher creates a test, they can share test_id along with password. In student's portal, students can take their exams by using their test-id and password.

Using XamTrack

We have a very easy to use platform, just a few clicks and you can create you test and can ask your students to take the test.
We are always there to help you. Ping us through email for support

Why to choose our platform ?

Advanced invigilation system

In Teacher's portal they have the access to the live webcam stream of all the students appearing for the test.
furthermore our system will let the invigilator know, who is the a suspected student by observing his activities.

Creating test is super easy

In teacher's portal faculties can prepare their question paper with our tool and set time limit for the test.

Appearing for test is simple

Students need to login through student's portal to access their dashboard there they just have to give test-id and password to take the test.
